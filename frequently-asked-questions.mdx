<Accordion title ="What is Bireme Lab?">

Bireme Lab is a company specializing in the creation of software and online services.

Our ambition is to provide **simple solutions** for **complex problems**, while **minimizing costs** to support companies and indiehackers in their development.

We have [written an article about the creation of Bireme Lab](https://bireme.io/en/blog/about-bireme-lab) which you can read to learn more about us and our motivations.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="What services does Bireme Lab offer?">

Our vocation is to **develop services and tools** to **support individuals and companies** in their operational strategies.

We are currently focusing on the creation of toolings dedicated for **mailing** and the distribution of editorial content.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="What is a bireme?">

A bireme was an ancient Greek warship renowned for its speed and agility, playing a central role in naval battles thanks to its speed and maneuverability.

It symbolizes our aspiration to evolve rapidly and adapt with ease. It illustrates our desire for freedom and our pursuit of entrepreneurial opportunities, like a "pirate ship" ready to explore and conquer new horizons.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="Why Lab in Bireme Lab ?">

The "Lab" appellation represents a strategic choice to **federate our offerings within an ecosystem** that will consolidate our image and embody a **gage of reliability**. All this, in an environment dedicated to research, design and innovation.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="How do I request the addition of a feature?">

We **love and encourage our users** to share their feedback with us. If you have any ideas and/or remarks that could help us improve our services you can make a [feature request via our dedicated channel](https://github.com/orgs/bireme-lab/discussions/new?category=ideas).

**Thank you so much** for helping us improve the Bireme Lab ecosystem!

</Accordion>
