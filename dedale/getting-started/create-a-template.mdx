## Create an email template

To create a new template, click on the "+ Create" button.

<img
  className="block"
  src="/images/create.webp"
  alt="To create a new template, click on the + Create button."
/>

A dialog will open letting you chose if you want to use a predefined template or start from a blank project.

<img
  className="block"
  src="/images/create-dialog.webp"
  alt="Choose between predefined template or starting from an empty one"
/>

An empty `Untitled` project will be added to the list of templates. You can open the `Code` panel to start editing the code.

<Warning>

Without creating an account, storage is carried out in your browser's data, with a maximum limit of 1 template. If you clean your history or browsing data, you risk losing your work.

You can [create a free account](https://bireme.io) to benefit from permanent storage.

</Warning>
