<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title ="What's Dédale?">

Dédale is a **free service** dedicated to companies and independants looking to create, develop and organize their mail templates with ease.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="Why did we create Dédale?">

We created Dédale in response to a problem we encountered during the development of Bireme Lab: how to optimize our workflow for creating, managing and sending emails for sales, marketing and transactional purposes.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="Who is Dédale for?">

In its first version, Dédale is aimed at developers who want to improve their email template development experience. Our short-term goal is to make the product accessible without any technical prerequisites.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="How does Dédale differ from React Email?">

Dédale relies on React Email for the development of email templates. Our aim is to improve the development experience around React email, such as with a better preview system or the ability to integrate variables. Full details of Dédale's features are available [on this page](/dedale/concept).

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="How to create an email template with Dédale?">

We've created a documention to help you [get started quickly with Dédale](/dedale/getting-started), with the aim of guiding you all the way to importing into your favorite mailing service.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="Can I integrate Dédale with my email service?">

With Dédale you can export your email template in `HTML` format, highly compatible with most existing professional tools.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title ="How can I request a new feature?">

We **encourage our users** to share their feedback with us. If you have ideas and/or comments that could help us improve our services, you can make a [feature request via our dedicated channel](https://github.com/orgs/bireme-lab/discussions/new?category=ideas).

**Thank you so much** for helping us improve the Bireme Lab ecosystem!

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
